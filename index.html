About Me

I'm Junjie Hu (胡俊杰 in Chinese). I received my BEng degree (2020) from Nanchang University (NCU). I am currently pursuing my PhD at the Institute of Information Engineering (IIE), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), with an expected graduation in 2026.

My main research interests are: AI/LLM for Network/Security, SDN & PDP.
